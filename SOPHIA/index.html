WAMALA GIRLS HOSTEL.

a paradise indeed!

Wamala Girls Hostel is a residential place located in Banda.
It was majorly constructed for students studying at Kyambogo University and all neighbouring universities.

Strictly only girls reside at this hostel. All friends and family are free to visit during the day but must
must be out by 8:00pm. Curfew time for residents is 12:00am.
We have a strong security team and all visitors can only access the hostel when they have an Identification Card with them.

The Hostel has gardens, a swimming pool, bar and restaurant where residents can host small celebrations
Swimming is free for residents and none residents have to pay a fee of UShs. 5000 only for swimming.
The hostel has wi-fi and secretarial services too all at fair prices.

You can't afford to miss out on this amazing place. A home away from home. a paradise indeed.
